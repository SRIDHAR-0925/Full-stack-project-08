Welcome to V.S.B. College of Engineering Technical Campus


V.S.B. Educational Trust was founded in the year 2000 by Shri.V.S.Balsamy, B.Sc., L.L.B., the founder and correspondent of the V.S.B. Engineering College, with an interest in promoting, managing and administrating educational institutions with high academic standards, discipline and to take up and help other allied activities in the field of education.
